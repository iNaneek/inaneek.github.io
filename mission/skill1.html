Additive Manufacturing & DFM

Addative manufacturing is the technology of tomorrow, allowing complex structures with less waste and labor.

FDM 3D printer design is at the foundation of understanding of the technology. Through personal projects with two VORON desktop 3D printers, I have been able to redesign several components to fulfill unique criteria while maintaining valuable aspects of the manufacturing process. Shown below are two toolheads I've designed to incorporate modified toolheads while maintaining all rigidity and functionality.

I translate my experience in 3D printer design into DFM skills for additive manufacturing. The design process gives me a very deep understanding of the manufacturing process, which I can apply when designing parts that will be made through additive manufacturing.
